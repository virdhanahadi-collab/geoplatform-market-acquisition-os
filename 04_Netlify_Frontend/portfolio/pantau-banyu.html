Product description

Pantau Banyu is a platform reference for coastal and water-related area views. It helps teams understand area context, risk information, and change indicators through clear map-based pages.

Use cases

1

Understand risk areas

Open coastal and water-related information in clearer map views.

2

Compare area context

Review boundaries, surrounding features, and risk-related indicators.

3

Support discussion

Use the platform as a shared view for area review and reporting.

FAQs

Can this be adapted for another project?

Yes. The platform structure can be shaped around different data, users, and project views.

Do users need GIS skills?

No. The page is designed so users can open maps, views, and reports without reading technical steps first.

Can data and research be linked?

Yes. Data can point to GISolution Data Hub, while research items can point to journal or publication pages.
